About Us

Deshi Bazar

Deshi Bazar is a local business aimed to provide customers with Bangladeshi, Indian, Pakistani, and Middle Eastern products at affordable prices. We offer the best spices for all your essential cooking needs. We also include a butchery where all our meats are halal.

Deshi Kitchen

Deshi Kitchen offers the best South Asain cuisine all at an affordable price. All our products are freshly made and halal. We also offer a wide variety of vegetarian options. We aim to serve our cutomers with the highest quality food all at a reasonable price. We are open to deliveries and caterings.
